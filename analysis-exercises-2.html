Definition 2.2.3 (Convergene of a Sequence). A sequence $(a_n)$ converges to a real number $a$ if, for every positive real number $\epsilon$, there exists an $N \in \mathbb{N}$ such that whenever $n \geq N$ it follows that $|a_n - a| < \epsilon$.
